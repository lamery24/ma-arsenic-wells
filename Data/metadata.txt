Data from several datasets were removed from this repository for privacy reasons or because the data is owned by others. Please contact the authors for information related to the following datasets:

well_parcels.csv: Join between the MassDEP Well Location Viewer (https://mass-eoeea.maps.arcgis.com/apps/webappviewer/index.html?id=cdd11842864942178b71f2c7bd5a0b95) and the MassGIS Property Tax Parcel Map (https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massgis-data-property-tax-parcels). Information describing how this was created using ArcGIS is presented in the manuscript.

ma_resi_parcels_pws.csv: Join between the MassGIS Property Tax Parcel Map (https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massgis-data-property-tax-parcels) and the internal MassDEP PWS Service Area Maps. This is a selection of all residential tax parcels within PWS service areas. Information describing how this was created using ArcGIS is presented in the manuscript.

ma_resi_parcels_wells.csv: Join between the MassGIS Property Tax Parcel Map (https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massgis-data-property-tax-parcels) and the internal MassDEP PWS Service Area Maps. This is a selection of all residential tax parcels outside of PWS service areas. Information describing how this was created using ArcGIS is presented in the manuscript.

sm_wells_as.csv: Join between the ma_resi_parcels_wells.csv and the internally created arsenic interpolation maps and the groundwater arsenic probability maps (Lombard, 2021: https://www.sciencebase.gov/catalog/item/5f2d4ce382ceae4cb3c2e1d6). Estimations of groundwater concentrations and probability exceedances at identified private well locations in the state.Information describing how this was created using ArcGIS is presented in the manuscript.

EAP_PWS Compiled List 11082022.xlsx: Internal MassDEP datasheet displaying PWSs in MA, owner information, and whether the PWS is in MWRA.

WellDrilling.xlsx: Full dataset of the EEA Well Drilling Data Portal (https://eeaonline.eea.state.ma.us/portal#!/search/welldrilling) downloaded on 10/20/2023.

pws-source-id.csv: Full dataset of public community surface and groundwater supply sources and public non-community supply sources as defined in 310 CMR 22.00 downloaded from MassGIS (https://www.mass.gov/info-details/massgis-data-public-water-supplies) on 9/21/2023.

Data in the wq_data folder was downloaded from the EEA Drinking Water Data Portal (https://eeaonline.eea.state.ma.us/portal#!/search/drinking-water) on 9/1/2023.
